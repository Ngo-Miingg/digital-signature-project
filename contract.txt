Hai ben cung nhau thoa thuan va ky ket hop dong hop tac kinh doanh voi cac dieu khoan sau day:

DIEU 1: NOI DUNG HOP TAC
1.1. Hai ben thong nhat cung hop tac de thuc hien du an/cong viec sau: [Mo ta chi tiet, ro rang ve noi dung hop tac, san pham/dich vu se cung cap, muc tieu can dat duoc].
1.2. Pham vi hop tac: [Xac dinh ro pham vi cong viec, khu vuc dia ly, hoac cac gioi han khac neu co].

DIEU 2: QUYEN VA NGHIA VU CUA CAC BEN

2.1. Quyen va nghia vu cua Ben A:
    - Nghia vu:
        + Cung cap/Dong gop [Von, nhan su, cong nghe, co so vat chat...].
        + Thuc hien cac cong viec cu the sau: [Liet ke cac dau viec Ben A chiu trach nhiem].
        + Dam bao tien do va chat luong cong viec thuoc pham vi phu trach.
    - Quyen:
        + Yeu cau Ben B thuc hien dung cac cam ket trong hop dong.
        + Duoc huong [Ty le %] loi nhuan sau thue tu hoat dong hop tac.
        + De xuat cac phuong an, giai phap de thuc hien tot noi dung hop tac.

2.2. Quyen va nghia vu cua Ben B:
    - Nghia vu:
        + Cung cap/Dong gop [Von, nhan su, marketing, mang luoi phan phoi...].
        + Thuc hien cac cong viec cu the sau: [Liet ke cac dau viec Ben B chiu trach nhiem].
        + Lap bao cao dinh ky [Hang thang/quy] ve ket qua hoat dong cho Ben A.
    - Quyen:
        + Yeu cau Ben A thuc hien dung cac cam ket trong hop dong.
        + Duoc huong [Ty le %] loi nhuan sau thue tu hoat dong hop tac.
        + Chu dong thuc hien cac cong viec trong pham vi duoc giao.

DIEU 3: CHI PHI VA PHAN CHIA LOI NHUAN
3.1. Tong chi phi du kien cho hoat dong hop tac la: [So tien] VND.
    - Ben A dong gop: [So tien hoac tai san quy doi tuong duong].
    - Ben B dong gop: [So tien hoac tai san quy doi tuong duong].
3.2. Loi nhuan tu hoat dong hop tac sau khi tru di tat ca cac chi phi hop le va thue se duoc phan chia theo ty le:
    - Ben A: [Ty le %]
    - Ben B: [Ty le %]
3.3. Viec phan chia loi nhuan se duoc thuc hien vao ngay [Ngay] cua thang/quy tiep theo.

DIEU 4: THOI HAN HOP DONG
4.1. Hop dong co hieu luc tu ngay [Ngay bat dau] den het ngay [Ngay ket thuc].
4.2. Hop dong co the duoc gia han neu ca hai ben dong y bang van ban truoc khi het han it nhat [So ngay] ngay.

DIEU 5: BAO MAT THONG TIN
Hai ben cam ket giu bi mat tuyet doi moi thong tin lien quan den noi dung hop tac, thong tin khach hang, bi mat kinh doanh... va khong tiet lo cho bat ky ben thu ba nao khac neu khong co su dong y bang van ban cua ben con lai.

DIEU 6: BAT KHA KHANG
Trong truong hop xay ra bat kha khang (thien tai, dich benh, chien tranh...), cac ben se tam hoan thuc hien nghia vu cua minh va se cung nhau ban bac giai phap khac phuc.

DIEU 7: GIAI QUYET TRANH CHAP
Moi tranh chap phat sinh tu hop dong nay se duoc giai quyet truoc het bang thuong luong, hoa giai. Neu hoa giai khong thanh, vu viec se duoc dua ra Toa an Nhan dan [Quan/Huyen, Tinh/Thanh pho] de giai quyet.

DIEU 8: DIEU KHOAN CHUNG
8.1. Hai ben cam ket thuc hien nghiem tuc cac dieu khoan da thoa thuan.
8.2. Moi sua doi, bo sung hop dong phai duoc lap thanh van ban va co chu ky cua ca hai ben.
8.3. Hop dong duoc lap thanh 02 (hai) ban co gia tri phap ly nhu nhau, moi ben giu 01 (mot) ban.

Hop dong co hieu luc ke tu ngay ky.
